Salted Caramel Apple Bread

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 can of buttermilk buscuits
  • 2/3 cup of white surgar
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 6 tablespoons of ground cinnamon
  • 2/3 cup apple butter
  • 2 tablespoons salted caramel sauce
  • flaky sea salt

Cooking Directions:

  1. Preheat over to 350F
  2. stir sugar and cinnamon butter into a bowl. Place melted butter in another bowl. Lightly coat each buscuit in butter then lightly coat with sugar mixture.
  3. Place biscuit on loaf pan and spread apple butter on another biscuit. Repeat. Sprinkle remaining cinnamon sugar over the loaf.
  4. Bake in over until biscuits are golden brown and toothpick inserted into center comes out clean. Should take roughly 30 minutes. Let cool for 5 minutes.
  5. Drizzle caramen sauce and sprinkle salt if desired.
