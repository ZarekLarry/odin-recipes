Making lasagna is a time consuming process. Sitting down with your loved ones to enjoy the fruits of your labor is well worth the wait.

Ingredients:

  • Italian sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Fresh Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Lasagna Step By Step:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook Noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna (meat sauce, noodles, cheese, meat sauce, cheese)
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let rest before serving

How Long to Cook

The lasagna should tak 50 minutes at 375 degrees. Cook covered for 25 minutes and the final 25 minutes uncovered. Then let it sit at room temp for 15 minutes before cutting into it.
